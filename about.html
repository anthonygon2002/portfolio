About Me

As he prepares to enter the next chapter, Anthony Gonzalez is ready to apply what he has learned in college to the real world. His goal is to obtain hands-on experience in any information technology (IT) position related to Cybersecurity.
Whether it's an internship or an entry-level position, he's prepared for the challenge. Anthony is about to graduate with his Associates in Cyber & Information Technology from Pasco-Hernando State College (PHSC).
Anthony has a background in computer languages such as Python and HTML, and he currently spends his time studying the interesting world of forensic tools and Ethical Hacking techniques. Learning is a path he has passionately established.
Anthony's education will continue once he graduates from PHSC. He plans to pursue a BAS in Cybersecurity at Saint-Pete College (SPC) to further expand his knowledge and experience in Cybersecurity.
